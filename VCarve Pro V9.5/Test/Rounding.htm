Create Rounding Toolpath Version 0.0
This gadget creates a toolpath to 'round' a square blank into a cylinder

NOTE: When post-processing, if the output is generated with the "Z Zero Position On Cylinder" set to "Surface of Cylinder", the surface will be the surface of the FINISHED cylinder. For rounding operations "Center of Cylinder" is a much safer choice.
